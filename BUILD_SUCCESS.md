## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### "App not installed" error
- Check you have enough storage (50 MB free)
- Verify "Unknown sources" is enabled
- Try restarting your device

### App crashes on launch
- Clear app cache and data
- Reinstall the app
- Ensure Android 5.0+ is installed

### Can't connect to other players
- All devices must be on same WiFi network
- Grant all requested permissions
- Disable VPN if active
- Check firewall settings

### Performance issues
- Close background apps
- Enable performance mode in settings
- Reduce graphics quality if needed
